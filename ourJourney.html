OUR JOURNEY



InGrid Express Cargo – A new age company into Air Freight and India’s leading Air Cargo Consolidator Company, embarked on its journey in the year 2022 starting its operations from Jaipur. A company promoted by Upendra Thagria & Dinesh Bairwa along with a committed group of industry professionals with over a decade of industrial exposure to the ever changing needs and demands of the discerning corporate world.


Understanding the highly competitive business environment where movement of tones of parcels and samples are a major requirement for all business establishments, most of them are very time sensitive and may carry high commercial nature for delivery under set time lines. InGrid Express Cargo was formed to create a most reliable express delivery module by working on a set of quality values everywhere across the Country.


Providing service and trust, we are now more committed to delight our customers by providing a friendly and fault free services. Constancy and unity of purpose is vital for achieving our mission of becoming the biggest and most reliable carrier network. We work continuously on improving profits through delighting customers by providing the fastest and most efficient express delivery services. Today we offer vide range of reliable products to meet changing demands of our customer’s at the most competitive and affordable commercials.
