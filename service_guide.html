SERVICE GUIDE


In the current competitive business world, the cargo packages need to be delivered with quick consideration due to personal or business exigency, especially when it comes to delivery of commercial – samples or packages, critical time sensitive articles, fragile and delicate articles, Air cargo services in India plays a vital role. In such cases, it becomes imperative to deliver cargo via fast and reliable mode and air transportation becomes the only way out for speedy movement.


Ingrid Express Cargo we have products which have been designed to help transport all such packages smoothly and safely from any part to any part of the Country.

Airport to Airport : This is a service where you can book your shipments through us at the airport (origin city) and collect the shipment from airport (destination city).


Door to Door : We do pick up of your shipment from your doorstep from the origin city and transport to the destination city with delivery at doorstep.


Fragile : These shipments are delicate in nature and need special handling where heavy weight over it or drop may damage.


Heavy Weight The shipment which is heavy in nature and cannot be lifted by porters. Such shipments need fork lift to lift and load.


Priority : These are time sensitive shipments where TAT has to be maintained. They can even be sample or export shipments, business conference or meeting, of event or trade fair, new business launch etc.


Passport : This is a very prime product. We extend this service to Individual, travel house, hotels etc. as fly on board courier (OBC) and delivery to Embassy address only.


Extra Baggage : This is a unique product which helps you to travel light. If you are on a family holiday or vacation and purchased some articles from tourist destination we carry it for you. Even if you are and intensive traveller and avoid carrying heavy baggage we provide door service.


Handle with Care : This is a product sensitive service. There are many such electronic goods which are to be transported straight as side turning or placing them upside down damages the product. Special label and handling is done.
